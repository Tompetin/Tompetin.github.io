Welcome to the Cyber Security Game!

Your mission: Learn and defend against cyber threats in an interactive way!

What is this game about?

In this game, you'll face challenges designed to teach you about cybersecurity threats and how to defend against them. Here's a sneak peek at what awaits you:

Level 1: Phishing Scams

Identify fraudulent emails and learn to protect your inbox.

Level 2: Password Defense

Test your ability to create and recognize strong passwords.

Level 3: DDoS Attacks

Take control as a game server admin and use given items to fight against DDoS attackers.
