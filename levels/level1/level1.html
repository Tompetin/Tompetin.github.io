Level 1: Phishing Awareness

Learn to differentiate between real and fake emails to protect your inbox.

In this level, you will encounter different emails, some of which are phishing attempts designed to trick you into revealing personal information. Your task is to examine each email carefully and decide whether it is legitimate or a phishing attempt. Look out for suspicious links, unusual sender email addresses, and urgent language. Stay alert and make the right choices to protect yourself from online scams!
